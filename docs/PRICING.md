# Price plans (India, INR)

Suggested subscription tiers for merchants/tenants. All amounts are in **INR**. Pricing is subject to change; treat this as a baseline for product and implementation. See [PRD.md](PRD.md) for monetization and Razorpay integration.

| Plan | Monthly (INR) | Target | Limits (example) | Features |
|------|----------------|--------|-------------------|----------|
| **Starter** | ₹999 – ₹1,499 | Small merchants, single-store | 1 program; up to 1,000 active members | Basic reporting; dashboard; email support |
| **Growth** | ₹2,999 – ₹4,999 | Growing D2C, multi-outlet | Multiple programs; up to 10,000 members | API access; better reporting; priority email |
| **Scale / Enterprise** | Custom | Large brands, aggregators | Higher limits; SLA | Priority support; SLA; optional white-label |

- **Trial:** 14-day free trial (Razorpay Subscriptions supports `trial_days`). Card may be required at signup with first charge after trial.
- **Billing:** Recurring monthly (or annual with discount, if offered). Razorpay Subscriptions; first payment and optional trial via Razorpay Checkout.
- **Currency:** INR only for India launch. Display may be GST-inclusive per Indian norms; document tax treatment in legal/checkout copy.

**Disclaimer:** These ranges are indicative. Final pricing, limits, and features are subject to product and business decisions.
